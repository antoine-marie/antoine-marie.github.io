Projects

Higher roots of the coupled-cluster equations

Prior to my second year Master degree internship, I did a bibliographical report about alternative formulations of single-reference coupled-cluster and the higher roots of the coupled-cluster equations.
I'm currently doing a six months internship under the supervision of Pierre-François Loos. The aim of this internship is to investigate the physical meaning of non-standard solutions of the alternative coupled-cluster formulations presented in my bibliographical report.

Perturbation theory in the complex plane

As part of the first year of my Master degree, I did a three months internship under the supervision of Pierre-François Loos. During this internship, we explored the links between he position of the energy singularities in the complex plane and the behavior of the energy perturbation series expansion. You can find my internship report here.
